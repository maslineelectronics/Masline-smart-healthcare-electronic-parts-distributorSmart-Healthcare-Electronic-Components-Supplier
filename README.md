Below is an overview of essential components commonly used in smart healthcare products and distributed or supplied by Masline:

- Heart rate and ECG sensors  
- SpO2 and blood oxygen modules  
- Temperature and body-heat sensors  
- Motion, respiration, and pressure sensors  
- Glucose monitoring sensor elements  
- Environmental and ambient condition sensors  

Such sensors support real-time patient tracking, diagnostic equipment, and wearable health devices.
